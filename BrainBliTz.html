BrainBliTz Competiton

Brain Blitz is a high school neuroscience competition destined for nurturing youth skills in neuroscience. It grants high school students the opportunity to showcase their talents and enrich their knowledge in neuroscience

Who Can Participate?

  • Students Enrolled in educational grades 9 12 (and their equivalent) in the 2024 2025 academic year, gap year students are eligible to participate.
  • Students from around the world are eligible to participate, with no restrictions regarding the student s nationality.
  • There are no fees associated with joining the competition.

Why Participate?

Stand Out Among Elite Students

Brain Blitz offers you the opportunity to showcase your skills and excel in a community of talented contestants.

Obtain Medals and Certificates

All participants in the first stage will obtain participation certificates in recognition of their efforts. Gold, silver, and bronze honors are awarded to finalists.

Gain Profound Knowledge in Neuroscience

IYNA October Chapter offers a neuroscience program, giving students access to inclusive materials and preparing them for the competition

Rules & Instructions

BrainBliTz has a zero-tolerance policy for cheating, giving rise to more equative competitiveness. Thus, contestants must adhere to some rules concerning this policy.

01

You must have stable internet connection to prevent any issues.

02

You must Be Ready and prepare your PC before the competition with 30 minutes at least.

03

You mustn't open any tab, only the tab of the competition.

04

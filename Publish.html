Rules and Criteria

01

Make sure the writing style of your submission is up to par and proofread it multiple times.

02

The article should be formatted in the IYNA format and follow our guidelines accordingly. Any failure to do so could result in your article being rejected for publication.

03

You are responsible for responding swiftly to comments and edits done by the supervising editors.

04

IYNA does not accept plagiarism in any form and takes it very seriously. If any plagiarism is detected in your submission, strict action shall be taken.
